Golf Darts

Hit the current hole only, throw three darts, and keep the lowest score possible. Best dart on each hole wins the hole for your scorecard.

Play the course, not the crowd.

9 holes: targets 1–9. 18 holes: targets 1–18.
Each hole = 3 darts. The app scores the best hit only from those three darts.
Double = 1 (hole in one), Triple = 2 (eagle), Inner Single = 3 (birdie), Outer Single = 4 (par), Miss = 5 (bogey).
Wrong number or bull counts as a miss for that dart unless bull is the current hole, which it never is in 9 or 18 hole mode.
